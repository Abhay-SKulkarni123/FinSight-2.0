Chart Analysis Guide

Understanding chart patterns and probabilities for different markets:

Chart patterns & probabilities

  • Head and shoulders: 85% probability of trend reversal when pattern completes
  • Double top/bottom: 75% probability of reversal, stronger confirmation needed
  • Triangle patterns: 60-70% probability of continuation in direction of trend
  • Flag/pennant: 80% probability of continuation after brief consolidation
  • Cup and handle: 70% probability of bullish breakout
  • Wedge patterns: 65% probability of reversal when pattern breaks

Technical indicators

  • RSI (14): Above 70 = overbought, below 30 = oversold
  • MACD crossover: 60-65% probability of trend continuation when signal line crosses
  • Moving averages: Price above MA(50) = 65% bullish probability, below = 65% bearish
  • Bollinger bands: Price touching upper band = 60% reversal probability, lower = 60% bounce
  • Volume confirmation: 75% probability of trend strength when volume supports price movement
  • Support/resistance: 70% probability of bounce at support, rejection at resistance

Probabilities are based on historical patterns and should be used alongside risk management strategies, not as guarantees.
